Tana Fellow Support System — Project Description
Company Context
Tana is a contractor placement company that places fellows (employees) with external partner companies in roles such as QA, data analysis, software development, and DevOps. Once placed, Tana remains responsible for all HR, support, and employee success functions. Communication happens via Slack (day-to-day) and Zoom (meetings with Talent Success Associates).

Problem Being Solved
Talent Success Associates (TSAs) manage multiple fellows simultaneously. Without a structured system, it's easy to lose context on each fellow's progress, miss blockers, and fail to follow up on commitments — especially during the critical onboarding 90-day period.

What We're Building
An AI-powered workflow tool that lives inside Slack with an external Talent Success Dashboard, designed to:

Parse end-of-day (EOD) updates submitted by fellows in Slack
Surface insights and action items for Talent Success Associates automatically
Support fellows through a smoother, well-supported 90-day transition


EOD Update Structure
Fellows submit daily updates with three components:

✅ Accomplishments — what they completed that day
🔜 Next Steps — what they plan to do next
🚧 Blockers — anything preventing progress (if any)


Core Features
1. Accomplishment Summarization

Extracts and summarizes each fellow's daily accomplishments
Generates a progress signal for the TSA (e.g. Stable / Needs Monitoring / Requires Attention)

2. Next Steps Tracking

Logs next steps from each EOD update
Cross-references against the following day's EOD to check whether listed next steps were completed
Surfaces discrepancies as follow-up items for the TSA

3. Blocker Escalation

Automatically flags any raised blockers
Adds them as explicit agenda points for the next scheduled meeting between the TSA and the fellow

4. Meeting Cadence Support

Supports the structured meeting frequency: 3x/week → 2x/week → 1x/week
Ensures TSAs walk into every meeting with relevant, pre-populated context per fellow

Goal
Ensure every Tana fellow has a well-supported, closely monitored 90-day onboarding experience — and that every Talent Success Associate has the right context to provide timely, meaningful support, regardless of how many fellows they're managing.
